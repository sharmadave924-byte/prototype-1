Nihilism

Nothing is real. Nothing has meaning. In the absence of objective truth, we find the ultimate, terrifying freedom.

Nihilism is the philosophical belief that suggests that life is without objective meaning, purpose, or intrinsic value. Moral nihilists assert that there is no inherent morality, and that any established moral values are abstractly contrived.

Core Tenets

  • Existential Nihilism: The theory that life has no intrinsic meaning or value.
  • Cosmic Nihilism: The view that the cosmos is vast, indifferent, and lacks any human-centric purpose.
  • Active Nihilism: The destruction of old values to clear the way for the creation of new ones.
