Stoicism

The art of enduring the storm. Focus on what you can control, and let the chaos of the world crash against you like waves upon an unyielding cliff face.

Stoicism is a philosophy of personal ethics informed by its system of logic and its views on the natural world. According to its teachings, as social beings, the path to eudaimonia (happiness, or blessedness) is found in accepting the moment as it presents itself.

Core Tenets

  • Dichotomy of Control: Understanding what is within our power and what is not.
  • Amor Fati: A love of fate; accepting everything that happens to us.
  • Memento Mori: Remembering our mortality to gain perspective on what truly matters.
