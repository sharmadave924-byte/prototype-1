Absurdism

Embrace the chaos. Laugh in the face of the unknown and find joy in the struggle itself. The boulder must be pushed, but you can choose to smile while doing it.

Absurdism is a philosophical perspective which holds that the efforts of humanity to find inherent meaning will ultimately fail (and hence are absurd) because the sheer amount of information as well as the vast realm of the unknown make total certainty impossible.

Core Tenets

  • The Absurd: The conflict between the human tendency to seek inherent value and meaning in life and the silent, cold universe.
  • Rebellion: Accepting the absurd and continuing to live and seek meaning in spite of it.
  • Freedom: The realization that since life has no ultimate meaning, we are free to create our own meaning in the present moment.
